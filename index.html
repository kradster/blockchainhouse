Blockchain House

Blockchain House is a real home for blockchain enthusiasts. We are the family sharing the passion for IT technologies and cryptocurrencies. In our case, these are not just empty words. Blockchain House’s crew isn’t only standing aside. We are a very active member of crypto-world and participate in every action taken by our crypto-society. Why? Because it is important to understand the problems our society may encounter on a daily basis and without fitting someone’s shoes, you can’t truly figure them out.
Creating projects like CoinDeal, Paycoiner or BuyCoinNow gave us a vast experience in blockchain and cryptocurrency industry. Each of our product was created neatly, starting with the marketing strategy and a product design, finishing it with choosing the best IT solutions to provide the complete software architecture. What’s more, we bet on complexity due to our motto:

"A synergy and trust are the two elements that make every project and business successful."
